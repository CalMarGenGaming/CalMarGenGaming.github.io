Social Events

CWG provides opportunities for students to unwind, relax, and meet new friends who share their interests. Here are some examples of past events:

LAN Parties

Queue up for a variety of multiplayer games with friends, including League of Legends, Overwatch, and Jackbox party games.

Weekly Study Jams and Game Nights

Get motivated to be productive or take a break from school with recurring casual events.

Themed Game Nights

Play or watch games to discover new favorites together; past themes have included dating sims, indie games, and games with female protagonists.

Non-gaming Social Events

In addition to playing games, meet other CWG members at events like Movie Nights or Speed Friending socials.
