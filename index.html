Cal Women in Gaming

Cal Women in Gaming (CWG) is a student-run club at UC Berkeley dedicated to empowering gender minorities in games—as players, competitors, and industry professionals. Our mission is to change what gaming looks like; we want every student who is touched by our program to feel confident that they belong to the gaming community.

Community

Looking to queue up in Overwatch, League of Legends, Valorant, and more? Want to discuss the latest industry news and game releases? Look no further! We foster safe, supportive communities in our Facebook group and Discord server. Positive vibes only :)

Social Events

During the academic year, we host weekly events where you can hang out with other gamers at UC Berkeley with common interests.

One-on-one Industry Mentorship Program

Get paired one-on-one with an industry mentor to learn more about the industry and advance your own career goals.

Professional Development

Interested in working in the gaming industry? Get career advice and learn about navigating the industry through events like resume workshops, industry Q&As, and studio tours.
