Frequently Asked Questions

Membership is free. Our events do not require you to pay anything; access to the Cal Esports Community Center is free as a part of our events. Groups of CWG members may have social events where they eat dinner together; we would not pay for those expenses, but these events are not mandatory and we have other events.

Our online events require internet access and we do not provide copies of paid games for you to play on personal devices. We try to hold a variety of events that include playing free games together, and if you don’t own a certain game you’re more than welcome to still join and watch!

You must be a UC Berkeley student that is a gender minority. Other than that, we welcome gamers of all skill levels and gaming interests who support our mission. However, we have members of our Discord server who are from other colleges or are not current students; these exceptions are determined on a case by case basis.
There isn’t any minimum number of events you’re required to attend for membership; feel free to join whenever you feel like!

No, feel free to join us anytime! However, signups for our one on one mentorship program and board applications typically occur at the beginning of the academic year (August/September).

We typically recruit new board members at the beginning of each academic year (August/September). Board applications will be shared on our social media.

The current most popular games among our members are League of Legends and Overwatch, but our members play a huge variety of games; we’re sure you’ll find someone here with some common interests.

Our Discord server has a strict verification system and we personally verify each and every person that wants access to our server.

Cal Women in Gaming is focused on the empowerment of gender minorities but supports empowerment on all levels. We ask that all members support this mission in good faith.

No, esports is handled through our parent organization Cal Esports. We do have several CWG members involved in the competitive scene, so feel free to reach out!
