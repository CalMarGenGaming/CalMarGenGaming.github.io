Community Spotlight #14

11/14/2023

Spotlight: Hannah

Affiliation: CMGG Member

Bio: Hellaurrr! I'm Hannah Lee, a super senior (4 and 1/2 years *whips) at Cal! I'm studying political economy and minoring in public policy. While my career interests lean more towards the public sector and legislation, I love to play games in my personal time! I play League (ironically and badly), Honkai Star Rail, and The Sims 4! Growing up, I gravitated towards story-rich games like Persona, The Walking Dead, The Wolf Among Us, (anything Telltale honestly), Harvest Moon (on Nintendo), and Tomb Raider (2013). I love getting attached to 2D characters and witnessing their growth and suffering :) If you've ever played any of these games, I'd love to talk and trash about them! The first and only game jam I've ever participated in was CMGG's 2022 Game Jam! I helped create a visual novel, which is about Cubbie, our mascot, who goes on a journey to recover their iconic headphones and console as well as to discover who they really are and where they belong. I created some of the art assets and wrote the storyline and dialogue for the visual novel. It was an amazing experience working with other passionate gamers and creators! I joined CMGG as a sophomore and the last 3 years with this club have been a wonderful experience. While I started out more as a discord lurker, I've participated in more in-person and discord events. In doing so, I've met many who share similar interests and identities, which has made gaming not only more fun but also social for me! Currently, I am in the mentorship program and while I have no plans to join the gaming industry, I have received incredibly insightful advice that is applicable to my own life and reassuring stories I can relate to in regards to my professional career. From the annual Halloween LANs at the esports center to the numerous coffee chats with industry professionals to the cute weekly discord events, I have created everlasting memories and so I have nothing but gratitude and pride for CMGG.
